Madhya Pradesh has many national parks which are a paradise for wildlife and nature lovers. Tigers, leopards, deer, and many bird species are found in these parks. Talking about human life, people living around national parks benefit from tourism and businesses related to wildlife conservation.

Madhya Pradesh, endowed with numerous historical heritages, attracts tourists from various parts with its incomparable natural wonders. The waterfalls situated here exceed every tourist's expectations in terms of their grandeur and beauty.
